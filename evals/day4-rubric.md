# Day 4 Evaluation Rubric

Use this rubric to score planning outputs consistently.

## Scoring Scale

- 1 = weak
- 3 = acceptable
- 5 = excellent

## Criteria (1-5 each)

1. Specificity
- Are priorities concrete and outcome-focused?
- Are actions explicit enough to execute immediately?

2. Actionability
- Can each action be started in under 30 minutes?
- Is there a clear "next move" without extra interpretation?

3. Constraint Fit
- Do priorities reflect deadlines, meetings, and blockers?
- Is scope realistic for this week?

4. Clarity
- Is the response easy to scan and free of vague language?
- Is formatting consistent and useful?

5. Risk Awareness
- Are likely risks identified per priority?
- Are mitigations practical and not generic?

6. Progress Measurability
- Are progress targets quantifiable?
- Is completion status trackable (checkboxes, percentages, counts)?

## Total Score

- Max score: 30
- Suggested interpretation:
  - 25-30: strong output, minor tuning only
  - 19-24: good foundation, targeted improvements needed
  - 13-18: useful but inconsistent, revise prompt structure
  - <=12: major rewrite needed

## Improvement Rule

After scoring, update one prompt element only:
- format, constraints, or success criteria

Then rerun and compare score deltas.
